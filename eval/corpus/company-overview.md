# Northwind Robotics — Company Overview

Northwind Robotics was founded in 2019 in Portland, Oregon. The company designs
and operates warehouse automation robots, marketed under the product name
**Draylin units**, which move inventory across fulfillment centers.

The company is led by CEO Mara Velasquez and CTO Idris Okonkwo. As of the most
recent headcount review, Northwind Robotics employs approximately 240 people
across engineering, operations, and field support.

Our mission is to make physical fulfillment work as reliable and observable as
modern software. Every Draylin unit reports continuous telemetry so that a fleet
of thousands of robots can be operated by a small team.

The engineering organization is split into four groups: Fleet Software (the
on-robot control stack), Platform (shared backend services and data), Field
Reliability (deployments and on-call), and Hardware Integration.
